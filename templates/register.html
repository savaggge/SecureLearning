Account Security

Creating a strong, unique password is your first line of defense in cybersecurity:

  • Use a different password for each of your accounts
  • Make your passwords long and complex
  • Consider using a passphrase: a series of random words with special characters
  • Never use personal information that could be easily discovered
  • Change your passwords regularly, especially after a data breach
  • Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong passwords
